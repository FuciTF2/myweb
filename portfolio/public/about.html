Who I am

I'm a developer based in the Czech Republic. I got into coding because I wanted to make things — games, tools, whatever — and it turned out writing code was the fastest way to do that. I don't stick to one thing. One week it's a web app, the next it's a game, the next it's something completely pointless but funny. That's kind of the point.

If you're looking for someone with a polished 5-year plan and a specialty, wrong place. If you're interested in someone who ships things and figures it out as they go, welcome.
