§ 01 — THEATRE OF OPERATIONS

Year: 1997. The geopolitical landscape is defined by fractured states, industrial collapse, and low-intensity conflicts kept deliberately off the record. The frozen nation of ESTIL maintains a fragile grip on order. The wasteland known as ASCANA maintains nothing. Decades of industrial exploitation left the soil toxic and the people abandoned — surviving through instinct, theft, and the understanding that no one is coming to help.

It is in Ascana that SUBJECT: BROOK was born. It is in Ascana that he learned what death looked like up close, long before he was ever given a weapon.

§ 03 — THE BERKSLEY UNIT RESTRICTED

BERKSLEY was not a training program. This distinction is important and should not be obscured in summary reports.

Children — selected, never recruited — were subjected to a systematic program of survival trials, chemical experimentation, and psychological conditioning. The stated objective: elimination of the fear response. The actual objective: proof of concept. The creation of an operative who functions without hesitation, without self-preservation instinct, without the parts of a human being that make them unreliable.

Casualty rate across all BERKSLEY cohorts: ██.█%. The survivors were not considered lucky. They were considered successful.

INCIDENT LOG — FIRST RECORDED TERMINATION

During Trial Phase 3, subject BROOK engaged and terminated a fellow participant in what reviewing officers classified as a defensive response. Subject displayed no observable distress response during or following the incident. Psychological assessment noted: "something has been permanently reorganised."

§ 05 — OPERATIONAL HISTORY

Subjects were deployed across ████ classified operations over a period of approximately ██ years. Survival rates on assigned missions were not factored into mission planning. Results were the only metric.

What subjects were not informed of: the missions were never the primary purpose. Every operation served as a data point. BERKSLEY was ongoing. They were still the experiment. They were always still the experiment.
